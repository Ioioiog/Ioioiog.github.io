Why Budgeting is Important for Recovery

Budgeting is a crucial skill for anyone recovering from gambling addiction. It helps you:

  • Gain control over your finances
  • Prioritize essential expenses
  • Build savings for emergencies
  • Develop healthy financial habits
  • Track progress towards financial goals

    Budgeting Tips for Recovery

    • Start with a simple budget and gradually add more detail
    • Track all expenses, no matter how small
    • Set realistic financial goals
    • Create an emergency fund to avoid financial stress
    • Review and adjust your budget regularly
    • Consider using the envelope system for cash expenses
    • Involve family members or trusted friends in your budgeting process
