Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have a gambling problem?

Signs of a gambling problem include preoccupation with gambling, needing to gamble with increasing amounts of money, and continuing to gamble despite negative consequences. Take our self-assessment quiz for a more comprehensive evaluation.

Is recovery possible?

Yes, recovery from gambling addiction is absolutely possible. With the right support, treatment, and commitment, many individuals have successfully overcome gambling addiction and rebuilt their lives.

How can I help a loved one with a gambling problem?

You can support a loved one by encouraging them to seek help, setting boundaries, and accessing resources for family members. Our Family Resources section provides comprehensive guidance on how to help while taking care of yourself.
